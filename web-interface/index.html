Course Utility

    Having a tough time making your 4-year plan for Rice when you don't even know when courses are offered? You're not alone.
    Some courses are offered only in the Fall or Spring, some every other year, and some just dropped off the face of the Earth.
    Look up your course in the search bar and see basic info at a glance and save yourself a headache :)

    If you're interested, you can look up the source code here to view the dataset and make something awesome!
